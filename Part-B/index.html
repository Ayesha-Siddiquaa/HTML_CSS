Monarch Butterfly

The Monarch Butterfly, scientifically named Danaus plexippus, is renowned for its vibrant orange and black wings, symbolizing beauty and resilience. These butterflies embark on an extraordinary migratory journey from North America to overwintering sites in Mexico or California, showcasing remarkable navigational skills.

Swallowtail Butterfly

The Glasswing Butterfly, scientifically named Greta oto, is known for its transparent wings, found in Central and South America. These delicate creatures excel in camouflage, seamlessly blending into their surroundings.

Blue Morpho Butterfly

The Blue Monarch butterfly, or Morpho menelaus, is known for its stunning iridescent blue wings found in Central and South American rainforests. Unlike its cousin, the Monarch, it doesn't migrate but gracefully flutters among lush vegetation.
